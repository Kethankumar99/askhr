LEAVE POLICY:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Employees get 20 sick leaves per year.
Sick leaves can be carried forward up to 10 days.
Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive sick days.
Casual leaves: 10 per year.
Annual leaves: 15 per year.
Maternity leave: 26 weeks for female employees.
Paternity leave: 2 weeks for male employees.

WORK FROM HOME (WFH) POLICY:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Employees can work from home 2 days per week.
WFH requests must be submitted 24 hours before.
Manager approval is required for WFH.
Emergency WFH can be requested same day.
Team meetings are mandatory even during WFH days.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Salary is credited on the 1st of every month.

Performance bonus up to 20% of annual salary.
Health insurance coverage of 5 lakhs for employees.
Provident fund as per government norms.
Yearly increment based on performance.
